Company Description

Devyani International Limited (DIL) is India's largest franchisee for Yum Brands, operating KFC, Pizza Hut, and Costa Coffee stores. Established in 1991, the company has grown to become one of the country's premier quick-service restaurant operators.

Key Brands:
  • KFC: 941 outlets in India (FY24)
  • Pizza Hut: 572 outlets
  • Costa Coffee: 150+ outlets
  • Vaango & other proprietary brands
Market Position:
  • Largest Yum Brands franchisee in India
  • Expanding aggressively into Tier 2 & 3 cities
  • International presence in Thailand (288 KFC stores)
  • Part of RJ Corp conglomerate (61.4% promoter holding)

Debt Strategy

Cash Sweep Mechanism: 50% of excess FCF (after mandatory amortization) used for voluntary prepayment. Accelerates deleveraging in later years as FCF grows. Maintains flexibility for strategic investments.
